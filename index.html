Who we are

Wearable architecture, engineered for the body

RAWx is a design studio engineering avant-garde leather harnesses that sit at the intersection of high fashion and unapologetic BDSM. Every piece is structural, striking, and cut to the body it’s built for — nothing off the rack.

We treat a harness as architectural armor: bold, precise, and made to command a room. Styled over lingerie or built into streetwear, a RAWx piece is designed to be seen. Custom detailing and precision adjustment are available on request, so every harness fits like it was forged for the wearer alone.

The RAWx standard

Built like architecture, finished like couture

All RAWx pieces are cut from premium, hand-selected leather and fitted with high-contrast, heavy-duty hardware. We treat every harness as wearable architecture — geometry and structural integrity come first, because that’s what lets a piece hold its shape and its edge.

Every rivet placement, every buckle finish, every stitch line is measured by hand. Nothing ships until it’s exact.
